The Battlefield Collection

Overview

Battlefield is a first-person shooter series of video games. The series is developed by Swedish company EA DICE and is published by American company Electronic Arts. The series features a particular focus on large maps, teamwork and vehicle warfare. The PC games in the series are mainly focused on online multiplayer. The Battlefield series has been played by more than 50 million players worldwide since 2012, this number continues to grow to this day.
