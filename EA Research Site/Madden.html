The History of Madden

Madden NFL was originally known as John Madden Football until 1993. it was developed by EA Tiburon for EA Sports. its has sold more than 130 million copies The games contain sophisticated playbooks and player statistics. As of 2013 the franchise has generated over $4 billion in sales. The franchise has 39 games in total. most if not all games can be played on every console.
